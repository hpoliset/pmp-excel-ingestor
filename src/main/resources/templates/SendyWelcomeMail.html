Thank you for attending one of our Heartfulness programs recently. We hope you enjoyed the experience of relaxation and meditation with yogic transmission. We would like to stay in touch with you and respond to your interest in meditation.

Yoga and meditation are now mainstream ideas with more research being done on their benefits every day. Meditating on the heart has its own advantages, and allows us to embrace a heart-based way of living. Heartfulness is feeling the lightness and joy of our true nature and experiencing it in our hearts. When we listen to those feelings and capture the inspiration that comes from within, we can master our life by listening to the heart. This exercise of fine-tuning the heart with the mind is done through Sahaj Marg yoga meditation. By meditating, we normalise our inner qualities and begin to excel in everything we do.

Heartfulness provides a stepwise scientific approach to meditation, where you experience the effects of yogic transmission directly and integrate the various aspects of the practice at your own pace, either in the comfort of your own home, in groups at colleges, workplaces and communities, and in our meditation centres.

So come let us relax and meditate.

Someone once asked the Buddha skeptically, "What have you gained through meditation."
The Buddha replied, "Nothing at all."
"Then, Blessed One, what good is it."
"Let me tell you what I lost through meditation: sickness, anger, depression, insecurity, the burden of old age, the fear of death. That is the good of meditation, which leads to nirvana."
